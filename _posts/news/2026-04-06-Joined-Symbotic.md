I’m excited to share that I’ve joined [Symbotic](https://www.symbotic.com) as a Principal Robotics Engineer.

Over the past several years at ClearMotion, I had the opportunity to work on RoadMotion, a preview active suspension control technology, contributing to motion planning and SLAM solutions. It has also been rewarding to see ClearMotion technologies make their way into mass production programs such as the NIO ET9. I’m grateful to have worked with such a talented team and to have been part of efforts bringing control technologies closer to real-world deployment.

At Symbotic, I’ll be working on control and automation for warehouse robotics systems. I look forward to applying my background in control, optimization, and applied mathematics, along with experience from automotive and autonomous driving, to this new domain. It’s also great to reconnect with a number of former colleagues and friends here.

Thanks to everyone who has supported me along the way — excited for what’s ahead.
